Mageganya Farm-stay

Located within the fertile farmlands of Nyandarua county, we are a farmstay that takes its pride in the peace and tranquility of the african countryside. At Mageganya, the greatest joy we have is to watch our guests take part in the activities which we run on the farm, and then partake of the farm fresh food that comes about as a result. All our meals are fresh and well worth the extra few minutes it takes to prepare them. Welcome and enjoy your stay with us.
